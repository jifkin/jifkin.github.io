Welcome!

I am a biomechanical engineering Graduate Research Assistant at the University of Virginia. Currently in my first year at the Center of Applied Biomechanics, I am interested in researching traumatic brain injury, impact biomechanics, and network neuroscience.

A little about my research

At both CAB and previously the University of Pennsylvania, I took a leading role on various projects regarding the brain. I've studied brain connectivity, different approaches to modeling impact, and neuroendovascular therapies. Click on one of the links to read more about my research.
